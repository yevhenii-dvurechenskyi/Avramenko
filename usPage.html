A series of books about art

Olesya Oleksandrivna Avramenko

Olesya Oleksandrivna Avramenko (born October 25, 1959 in Zaporizhzhia) is a Ukrainian art historian, curator, art critic, and candidate of Art History (1993). She is a member of the National Union of Artists of Ukraine (1988) and a distinguished figure in the arts of Ukraine (2010). Avramenko has received two Silver Medals from the National Academy of Arts of Ukraine (2012, 2016). She is also a member of the international art critics association AICA (2018). Avramenko is the author of more than three hundred publications on art and artistic life in Ukraine during the 20th-21st centuries. She has also created concepts and organized approximately one hundred art exhibitions and projects.

ZARETSKYI

Author: Olesya Avramenko

Description: The monographic study of the work of the Ukrainian artist Viktor Zaretskyi (1925-1990) is written on the basis of an analysis of his creative output, personal meetings of the author of the book with the artist in 1987-1990, archival materials and interviews with colleagues and friends of the artist. The monograph can be a source not only for studying Viktor Zaretskyi's work, but also for understanding the artistic life and awareness of cultural and spiritual processes in Ukraine in the second half of the 20th century.
